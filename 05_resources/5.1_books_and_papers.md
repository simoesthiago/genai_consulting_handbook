# Curated Books and Papers on Generative AI

This section provides a curated list of essential reading materials for Generative AI consultants, ranging from strategic business guides to technical research papers.

1) Rewired: The McKinsey Guide to Outcompeting in the Age of Digital and AI, Eric Lamarre, Kate Smaje, Rodney Zemmel ([Link](https://www.amazon.com/Rewired-McKinsey-Guide-Outcompeting-Digital/dp/1119932035))
2) All-in On AI: How Smart Companies Win with Artificial Intelligence, Tom Davenport, Nitin Mittal ([Link](https://www.amazon.com/All-Smart-Companies-Artificial-Intelligence/dp/164782487X))
3) Human + Machine: Reimagining Work in the Age of AI (Expanded Edition), Paul R. Daugherty, H. James Wilson ([Link](https://www.amazon.com/Human-Machine-Reimagining-Work-Expanded/dp/1647890333))
4) Power and Prediction: The Disruptive Economics of Artificial Intelligence, Ajay Agrawal, Joshua Gans, Avi Goldfarb ([Link](https://www.amazon.com/Power-Prediction-Disruptive-Economics-Intelligence/dp/1647824195))
5) Practical Path + People = AI Outcomes, Tim Creasey ([Link](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/practical-path-people-ai-outcomes-tim-creasey-ce6gc))
6) HBR Guide to Generative AI for Managers, Harvard Business Review ([Link](https://www.amazon.com/HBR-Guide-Generative-Managers-Books/dp/1647827879))
7) Competing in the Age of AI, Marco Iansiti & Karim R. Lakhani ([Link](https://www.amazon.com/Competing-Age-AI-Leadership-Algorithms/dp/1633697622))
